CSS News

Courtice Secondary School takes pride in their school community, bringing people together in a variety of events. Everything from Haunted Halls, to Semi Formals, to Relay for Life! Let's take a closer look at our events!

News Reporting

Our schools amazing grade 11 communication technology's class reports weekly on events happening around the school through Courtice: This Week news segments. The Courtice Chronicle is our school paper, with quarterly issues reporting the top stories.

Terry Fox Walk

The Terry Fox Walk is an event participated in by over 10,000 schools in Canada to raise money for the Terry Fox Foundation. Courtice Secondary School participates annually, and has raised thousands of dollars in support of cancer research.

Haunted Halls

Haunted Halls is a school event that brings the school community together, embracing the spirit of Halloween in Courtice Secondary's very own haunted house.

Winter Semi-Formal

The Winter Semi-Formal is the first dance of the year, allowing students a chance to dress up and show off their moves in a themed dance, planned and executed by Courtice's leadership class.

Merry Menagerie

The Merry M is the school's annual talent show, showcasing the talent hidden within Courtice Secondary School. This is also the scene of the unveiling of Courtice Secondary's famous staff video, where teachers take trends from the past year and incorporate them into a hilarious video for the students.

Co-Prime Elections

Co-Prime Elections take place in May, allowing the student body to vote for their next years co-prime ministers. Students running in the election will campaign, starting about a week before the election day.
