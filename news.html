CSS News

Courtice Secondary School takes pride in their school community, bringing people together in a variety of events. Everything from Haunted Halls, to Semi Formals, to Relay for Life! Let's take a closer look at our events!

News Reporting

Our schools amazing grade 11 communication technology's class reports weekly on events happening around the school through Courtice: This Week news segments. Student-favourite segments include teacher features, and Hallway Johnny. Our communications technology class works incredibly hard every week to get amazing news reports out to the school. The Courtice Chronicle is our school paper, with quarterly issues reporting the top stories.

Terry Fox Walk

The Terry Fox Walk is an event participated in by over 10,000 schools in Canada to raise money for the Terry Fox Foundation. Courtice Secondary School participates annually, and has raised thousands of dollars in support of cancer research. The day of the Terry Fox Walk, there are many fun games for students to participate in after the school-wide walk, and a barbeque for students to enjoy a free hot dog (with vegetarian and halal options).

Haunted Halls

Haunted Halls is a school event that brings the school community together, embracing the spirit of Halloween in Courtice Secondary's very own haunted house. Actors include talented students from the leadership class, and some recruited teachers from around the school.

Winter Semi-Formal

The Winter Semi-Formal is the first dance of the year, allowing students a chance to dress up and show off their moves in a themed dance, planned and executed by Courtice's leadership class.

Merry Menagerie

The Merry M is the school's annual talent show, showcasing the talent hidden within Courtice Secondary School. This is also the scene of the unveiling of Courtice Secondary's famous staff video, where teachers take trends from the past year and incorporate them into a hilarious video for the students. The Merry M marks the end of school before winter break, and stirs up holiday spirit in everyone.

Student vs Teacher Basketball Game

The Student vs Teacher Basketball Game is an amazing event that raises school spirit, incorporates friendly competition, and raises money and awareness for Epilepsy Durham, a charity that supports research into epilepsy.

Spring Semi-Formal

The Spring Semi-Formal is a great way for students to dress up and have fun with their friends in a themed night planned by the grade 11 leadership class.

Co-Prime Elections

Co-Prime Elections take place in May, allowing the student body to vote for their next years co-prime ministers. Students running in the election will campaign, starting about a week before the election day. All fighting for a chance to help lead and direct the next year's leadership class.
