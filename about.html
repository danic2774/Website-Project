About Courtice Secondary

Address: 1717 Nash Rd, Courtice, ON, L1E 2L8

Founded: September 1960

Grades: 7-12

Principal: Jeff Watt

School Board: Kawartha Pine Ridge District School Board

Programs

Specialist High Skills Major

  • Arts and Culture
  • Construction
  • Environment
  • Information and Communications Technology

Many students desire to specialize in certain areas of work, and the Specialist High Skills Major program allows students to recieve extra education in an area of their choosing. Students will be provided many free oppourtunities (funded by the government of Ontario) and certifications, that could regularly cost students, like CPR certification.

French Immersion

Courtice Secondary offers a continued French immersion program for students, preparing students academically for their DELF test, allowing many students to graduate secondary school bilingual.

Co-operative Education

Students have the oppourtunity to take several co-operative education periods in their class schedules, allowing students to learn valuable on-the-job skills and acquire some excellent work experience.

Special Education

Courtice Secondary prioritizes education and learning for all, as demonstrated by the wonderful special education program. The program foundation is the Learning Life Skills classes, which teach valuable and essential life skills to students!
